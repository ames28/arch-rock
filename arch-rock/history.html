How we started


We started out in 1967 when our founders, Lidia & Fred Mercer, opened a small ice cream window alongside their friends grocer in Brighton.

It was a very popular little ice creamery that Lidia & Fred opened a larger eat in location just down the road.

Our Products


After some years selling a small selection of flavours, Arch Rock branched out to have a wider selection on offer alongside the expanding business.

After testing the flavours and varying locations a set menu of 10 flavours was established, in hope that anyone would find a flavour.

Ice Cream Van


Along with the expansion of Arch Rock locations, we also introduced some ice cream vans that go out the beaches during summer.

This allowed us to reach more people while they were out enjoying the beach.

A New Era


Lidia & Fred's grandsons, Mason & Declan, now run the family business.

They have started to expand Arch Rock into the United States, with the intention of branching out into other territories in the near future.
